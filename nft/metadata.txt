DESCRIPTION
"Pioneers: Unlocking Life's Trajectory" – Using interaction to transform "overcoming life's obstacles" into a tangible, audible, and touchable artistic experience, helping you move from confusion to clarity.

This is an interactive artwork. Use the keyboard to explore the painting:

W/S — up/down drift
Q/E — rotate (spiral warp)
A/D — tilt (directional smudge)
R — reset

After 30 seconds without input, the piece enters a gentle auto-animation mode. Press any control key to interact again.

Created by Lu, Smile, and Ruby as part of MDS221 – Interactive Multimedia Installation, University of Saint Joseph (USJ), Macau.
